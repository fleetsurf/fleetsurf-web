The big idea

No agent should
surf alone.

Every AI tool today works in its own silo — its own context, its own memory, its own lane. Fleetsurf is the open layer that lets them ride together.

What it is

One protocol.
Every agent.

Fleetsurf is a universal adapter for AI agents. Connect the setup you already love — then talk to every other tool, runtime, and agent through one open, neutral standard.

The ecosystem

Plug in everything.

Every agent and coding tool, connected through one layer — routing context, calls, and results between them in real time.
